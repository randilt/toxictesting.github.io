about Kenshinkai Dojo 道場

Kenshinkai Dojo 道場 is a premier karate training center located in Sri Lanka, offering comprehensive instruction to students of all levels. Our experienced and talented trainers are dedicated to guiding each student on their journey to becoming a champion. Our classes are designed to be fun, challenging, and rewarding, and we use a variety of techniques and exercises to help our students improve their skills and reach their full potential. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, we invite you to join us at Kenshinkai Dojo and experience the benefits of karate for yourself.
